## Featured Projects

### [Volve Oil Field Production Anomaly Detection](https://github.com/AlliMariam/volve-production-analysis)

Unplanned downtime in North Sea oil operations can cost over $1M per day. I analyzed 8 years of production data from Equinor's Volve field to understand what anomalous production looks like

**What I found:**
- Flagged 382 anomalous production days across 5 wells
- 70% of anomalies occurred in the first 18 months of operation
- Late-life wells showed deviations 15% higher than baseline before failure

**Built with:** Python, Isolation Forest, Power BI, pandas

**Key learning:** Understanding the domain matters as much as the model. I spent significant time learning how oil production actually works before the analysis made sense.

---

### 🏥 [NHS A&E Demand Forecasting](https://github.com/AlliMariam/healthcare-resource-optimization)

Winter bed shortages cost the NHS hundreds of millions annually. I built a model to predict emergency admissions 6 months ahead using 10 years of NHS England data.

**Results:**
- 85% accuracy in predicting admission volumes
- Quantified winter surge: 20-25% higher than summer baseline
- Identified regional variation: Northern England peaks 4 weeks earlier than the South

**Built with:** Python, Random Forest, time series feature engineering

**Impact:** The model shows winter pressure is predictable - we just need to act on the forecasts.

### Data Readiness Diagnostic Framework *(MSc Dissertation)*

Most predictive maintenance projects fail because of data quality issues, not poor models. I'm building an automated diagnostic tool that checks industrial sensor data before any ML work begins.

**The problem:** Engineers at Shell, Chevron, and TotalEnergies all described the same issue: they can't trust their sensor data enough to deploy predictive models confidently.

**What the tool does:**  
Runs automated checks for sensor drift, missing data patterns, timestamp inconsistencies, and feature correlation breakdown.

**Testing on:** NASA C-MAPSS turbofan degradation dataset

**Built with:** Python, statistical testing, pandas, NumPy, scipy

**Timeline:** Completing May 2026. Planning to open-source after submission.
